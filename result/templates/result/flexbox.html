User's Guide

Step 1: Use Notepad or any other text editor to prepare a file containing student names, test scores, attendance scores, asignment scores and exam scores e.g (TUNDE OLUMO, 20, 10, 10, 60) in a new line for each students. Then save it with a file name e.g my_file.txt. When there are two gender classes, just separate the class A from class B by leaving an empty line. Click here for sample.

Step 2: Login or Sing-up (new users) into your account. Click on the plus-sign in the left navigation bar. Then, locate your prepared txt file. Upload it by simply click upload!

Step 3: Complete your upload by chosen the approprate class, term, subject from the dropdown lists. Provide the subject teacher name in the space provided. Then, save to proceed. You will be redirected to your DASHBOARD

Note: On the DASHBORAD, you can navigate to various uploaded files through the blue links. With this, class subject can be deleted once or by each student. Student name can be edited by clicking the link to the targeted name. If you need to adjust score(s), the txt file need to be re-edited, re-saved and re-loaded, this is done after deleting the whole class affected by simply click the star-sign . After comfirmig deletion the old records will be removed.

Step 4: On successful completion of these steps above, scrow down to locate the download link . The processed result can be downloaded in csv format for any further uses.

Step 5: Annual records can be generated once third term results were successfully uploaded. After download of third term result, the annual result can be generated from the same page by clicking the mark sign .

Step 6: By clicking on overall result, first and second term results can be edited to remove the left students before generating annual overall scores. Only students with third term and any other score should remain before clicking on generate result on the template.
